Hello Everyone!

We understand that losing something can be stressful, but with our user-friendly interface and dedicated team, we aim to make the process of reclaiming lost items as smooth and efficient as possible.At the heart of the IGDTUW Lost and Found project is the belief that small acts of kindness can have a big impact. By taking a few moments to report a lost item or to submit information about a found item, you're not only helping someone retrieve their belongings but also contributing to a culture of empathy and solidarity within our community.

Our platform serves as a digital hub where students, faculty, and staff can come together to help each other in times of need. By reporting lost items with detailed descriptions and locations, and by keeping an eye out for found items, we can all play a role in making our campus a more caring and connected environment.
